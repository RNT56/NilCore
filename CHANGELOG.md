- **P8-continue-failed** — A model-chosen **"continue a failed attempt"** capability for the supervisor: when a subagent did useful-but-incomplete work (ran out of road, or a fixable error — not the wrong approach), the supervisor can RETRY it building on its partial work instead of re-deriving from scratch.
